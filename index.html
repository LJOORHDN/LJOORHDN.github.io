Game Design Projects


Kinect Air Guitar:




This prototype game played off the fun idea of playing an air guitar. Using the Microsoft Kinect device and Unity’s engine, I coded a music rhythm game where you can rock out with an air guitar. The game tracks the player’s body and overlays it with a guitar on screen. Players strum using their hands on the guitar, matching the color coordinated numbers or rocking out in freestyle.


Xtreme Dodgeball:




Using their iPhones, players compete against waves of dodgeball teams in Xtreme Dodgeball. Using the iPhone’s gyroscope players tilt their phones to dodge incoming balls thrown by the opposing team. After picking up a ball they can swipe at their screen to throw the ball across the court. I coded the entire game including player control, enemy AI, and dodgeball physics using Apple’s programming language Swift.



Only Human:





In the distant future, the last remnants of humanity have been forced to flee the Earth as the planet has been destroyed. As the commander of one of the last remaining faction fleets, the future of the human race lies in your hands. Manage your resources and build your fleet up to protect yourself from other factions who are doing the same. While building trust with other commanders, remember to watch your back since others will do what it takes to survive. As the lead game designer of this card game, my job was to create and balance every ship which are the main focus of the game.
